Address Lookup

Please enter your address to verify residency within the district and click "Go to the Next step" when complete.
Address as entered must be recognized by the United States Postal Service (USPS) as a valid address in their database.
If necessary, please indicate the unit number using "unit," "apt" or "apartment."
Please do not use special characters such as #, @, or any other non-alphanumeric symbols, except for periods (.), commas (,), dashes (-), and slashes (/).
If uncertain, check your address on the U.S. Postal Service website.
