ABOUT JOSH

Josh Harder knows his job is to fight for every day families. Josh first ran for office to stand up to politicians threatening to take his brother’s healthcare away.  Now, Josh proudly represents California's 9th Congressional District, covering San Joaquin County as well as parts of Stanislaus and Contra Costa counties.
