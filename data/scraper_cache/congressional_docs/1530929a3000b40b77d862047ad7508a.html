Rep. Sykes will always stand up for our community in Congress.
Rep. Sykes will always work to make sure our local community is supported in Congress, and that means standing up for our legacy as the Rubber Capitol of the World. Rep. Sykes offered two amendments to the National Defense Authorization Act that would ensure #OH13 remains a leader in rubber and polymer technologies.
