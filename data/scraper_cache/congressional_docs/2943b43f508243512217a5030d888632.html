Tour Request

Visitors to Washington, D.C. have the opportunity to get tickets through my office for tours of the White House and FBI. Please fill out your zip code information below to access the White House/FBI Tour Request Page The White House is available for tours from Tuesday- Saturday. The White House may be closed to tours for holidays and special events. White House tours are currently experiencing high levels of demand and we cannot guarantee tickets for any visitors. If you are interested in a White House tour, please submit your request at least three months before your requested tour date.

Once you have submitted a White House tour request please look out for an email from the White House (noreplytours@who.Eop.Gov) requesting every guest to register and provide their information. This email maybe filtered to your spam folder so please check all inboxes. You MUST fill out this form within 36 Hours of receiving it or your tour will be canceled by the White House.

Visitors will be unable to access the White House Complex beginning May 7, 2025, without a valid form of government-issued ID: a REAL ID or a passport. Non-REAL IDs will no longer be accepted for any guests. Any guest who is unable to present a REAL ID or a valid passport will not be admitted to the White House Complex.

The FBI Experience is open to tours on Monday- Friday, excluding Federal Holidays. There may be unforeseen operating delays and/or closures of the JEH building, that will cause tours to be unavailable on certain dates. Visitors must be either US citizens or hold a valid Green Card to attend the FBI Tour.

You can also reserve tickets on your own behalf to other DC attractions:

  • U.S. Capitol, please visit https://tours.visitthecapitol.gov/ (groups of 10 and under) 
  • Visit https://groups.visitthecapitol.gov/ (groups over 10) 
  • Self-Booking a Kennedy Center Tour https://www.kennedy-center.org/visitor/) (walk-ups are welcome) 
  • Self-Booking a Library of Congress Tour (http://www.loc.gov/visit/) (walk-ups are welcome) 
  • Self Booking a Supreme Court Tour ( http://www.supremecourt.gov/visiting/visitorservices.aspx) (walk-ups ONLY) 
  • Self-Booking a Pentagon Tour (https://https://www.defense.gov/Pentagon-Tours) (Advance booking is REQUIRED) 
  • Self-Booking Bureau of Engraving and Printing (https://www.bep.gov/visitor-centers/washington-dc-tour-and-visitor-center/tour)
  • Self-Booking the Washington Monument (https://www.nps.gov/wamo/planyourvisit/fees.htm)
