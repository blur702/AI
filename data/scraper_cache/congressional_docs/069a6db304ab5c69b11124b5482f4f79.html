Votes and Legislation

One of the most important aspects of Congress is developing, making, and monitoring laws. New legislation is passed almost every week. Please contact our office to share your thoughts on current legislation that may affect you, your family and your community.

Learn more about legislation sponsored and co-sponsored by Congressman Veasey.

Constituents can view the U.S. House of Representatives' latest votes on Capitol Hill and view Roll Call Votes by the U.S Congress. Learn more About Legislation of the U.S. Congress and Votes in the House and Senate.
