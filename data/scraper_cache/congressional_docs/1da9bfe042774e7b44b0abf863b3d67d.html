Military Academy Nominations

The application window for Congressman Olszewski's nomination to attend the academies in Fall of 2026 has closed.
 

The Congressman's service academy nomination application closed on October 3rd at 5PM. If you believe you applied in time you should have received an email from our team on October 7th. If you have not received this email and believe you should have, please reach out to: lindsey.macgregor@mail.house.gov
Interviews will be virtual and take place on Friday October 24th.

Instructions

The honor of attending a service academy comes with the obligation and commitment to serve in the military for a minimum of five years upon graduation. For more information, please review the page of Frequently Asked Questions for those seeking a Military Academy Nomination.

Members of Congress may nominate candidates for appointment to four of the five U.S. service academies: U.S. Military Academy (USMA), West Point, NY; the U.S. Naval Academy (USNA), Annapolis, MD; the U.S. Air Force Academy (USAFA), Colorado Springs, CO; and the U.S. Merchant Marine Academy (USMMA), Kings Point, NY. The fifth service academy, the U.S. Coast Guard Academy (USCGA), New London, CT, does not require a congressional nomination for appointment.

Representative Olszewski may nominate up to fifteen individuals for each vacant academy slot allotted to our district, except for the Merchant Marine Academy where he can only nominate up to ten allotted slots. To request a Military Academy Nomination from our office, please complete the form below. Best wishes to you as you pursue your goal of attending one of the U.S. Service Academies and serving our country.

The application process for Congressman Olszewski's nomination to attend the academies in Fall of 2027 has not begun. Please complete the form below once our application process begins. When you hit submit it will turn into a PDF file and prompt you to print, sign, scan and email this application to MD02Academy@mail.house.gov, or deliver it in person to Congressman Olszewski's Towson Office. Hitting submit alone, does not enter your application, you must email it or deliver it to the office.
 
Office of Representative Johnny Olszewski
Attn: Service Academy Nomination
110 West Road Suite 420
Towson, MD 21204
 
Please include with your application:  a current photo of yourself, your high school transcript, three letters of recommendation, and your resume including extra curricular activities, work experience (paid and unpaid), and leadership experience.
The application deadline has not yet been released.
