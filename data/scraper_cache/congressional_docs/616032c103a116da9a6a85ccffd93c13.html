Priorities for Ohio

America First: A Commitment to Our Nation

I am unwavering in my commitment to an America First policy across every level of government. I oppose endless foreign wars, job offshoring, and policies that prioritize others over our own citizens. Securing our borders and ending illegal immigration are non-negotiable our people must come first.

I am dedicated to cutting through bureaucratic inefficiencies and enforcing fiscal responsibility in our federal agencies. As a proud member of the DOGE Caucus, I am committed to reassessing and eliminating wasteful federal programs. Taxpayer dollars must be spent wisely, aligned with our nation’s true priorities. It’s time to demand accountability and efficiency from Washington; no more reckless spending, no more excuses.

Learn More

Economic Growth & Workforce Empowerment

A strong America depends on a thriving economy driven by innovation and opportunity. I am committed to lowering taxes and cutting burdensome regulations to fuel economic growth and job creation. We must support our native industries and rebuild the manufacturing strength that once defined our region.

A business-friendly environment is essential to attracting investment, encouraging entrepreneurship, and securing long-term prosperity for future generations. Additionally, we must protect American industries from unfair foreign trade practices, ensuring that our workforce and businesses are always the top priority. It’s time to restore America’s economic dominance and put our people back to work.

Learn More

Unleashing American Energy

America must take the lead in energy production to secure our independence and strengthen national security. I support policies that expand domestic energy development including oil, natural gas, and clean coal, ensuring reliable and affordable energy for all Americans.

By prioritizing traditional energy sources in our budget reforms, we can reduce reliance on foreign energy, create jobs, and drive economic growth. Lower energy costs mean lower prices for goods across the board, easing the financial burden on American families and businesses. A strong energy sector is the backbone of a strong nation it’s time to unleash America’s full potential.

Learn More

Make America Healthy Again

Every American deserves access to affordable, transparent, and efficient healthcare. I am fighting for pricing transparency to empower consumers, drive competition, and lower costs across the board. No one should be blindsided by hidden fees or inflated medical bills.

We must also take aggressive action to lower drug prices and eliminate wasteful spending in our healthcare system. Cutting bureaucratic inefficiencies will save taxpayers billions and ensure resources go where they are needed most. It’s time to put patients first and make healthcare work for the American people not special interests.
