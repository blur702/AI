Serving You



Help with a Federal Agency

If you cannot get an answer from a federal agency in a timely fashion or if you feel you have been treated unfairly, our office may be able to help.



Service Academy Nominations

The honor of attending a service academy comes with the obligation and commitment to serve in the military for a minimum of five years upon graduation.



Visit DC

Our office can request tickets to some Washington, D.C. attractions for residents of Iowa’s Third District. All tickets are free and provided on a first-come, first-served basis.



Letters of Support for Federal Grants

Our office will review grant support letter requests from residents of Iowa’s Third District who are applying for a specific federal grant. Please complete this form found on this page to be considered.



Internships

Our office is accepting applicants for internships in both our Washington, DC and district offices.



Commendations and Greetings

If you would like to request a Congressional Commendation or a Presidential Greeting for someone living in Iowa’s Third Congressional District please submit the form found on this page.



Congressional App Challenge

Find out more about the Congressional App Challenge, which is a competition for middle and high school students to learn how to code by creating their own applications.



Art Competition

Learn more about the Artistic Discovery Contest open to all high school students that live in our Congressional District. You may also fill out an entry form to send to our office.



Purchase a Flag

Purchase U.S. flags through our office. You may also request the flags purchased through our office be flown over the Capitol.
