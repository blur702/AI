D.C. Tours and Tickets

  • Home
  • Services
  • D.C. Tours and Tickets
  • Print

United States Capitol and House and Senate Office Buildings: House office buildings are open to the public. Capitol tours are available through our office and through the Capitol Visitors Center.

Library of Congress: Timed and ticketed entry passes are required. Self-guided tours are available.

White House: White House tours are available from 8 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. between Tuesday and Saturday. Please submit tour requests at least three months in advance.

FBI Building: FBI Building tours are available from 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. between Monday and Friday. Please submit your tour requests at least three months in advance.

U.S. Treasury: There are limited tours available to the public on the first and third Saturdays of the month at 9 a.m. and 10:30 a.m.

Supreme Court: The Supreme Court Building is open to the public Monday – Friday from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m. for self-guided tours.

Bureau of Engraving and Printing: Up to 9 free timed-entry tour tickets may be reserved at a time. Tours are open Monday through Friday: 9 a.m. - 2 p.m., with the last tour at 2 p.m.
